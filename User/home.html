INNER SPARC

Inner SPARC Realty is a real estate investment company that specializes in helping clients find and acquire profitable real estate properties. Our team is made up of experienced real estate investors, brokers, and property managers who have a deep understanding of the market and the various investment opportunities available.

Inner SPARC Realty Corporation began as a small dream and quickly grew into a thriving business, thanks to the trust of our valued clients. Founded on October 14, 2019, by Licensed Real Estate Broker Gabriel Villamor Libacao Jr., REB, and Mr. Erwin G. Baguioan, Inner SPARC transitioned from a humble sole proprietorship into a corporation driven by a passion for helping Filipinos achieve homeownership.
